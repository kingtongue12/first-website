ANNOUNCEMENTS

On May 16-17 2024, Sandayaw will hold its kick-off concert titled, "Muli, Sandayaw: Manumbalik sa Indak ng Pusong Sabik" in Charles Fuller Baker Hall.

The concert aims to showcase the different folk dances from the Philippines. It is focused on promoting the beauty and significance of these dances. Thus, it encourages the recognition of the culture and tradition it entails and its development through time.
